The Pop Shop

- Haring opened the Pop Shop in downtown Manhattan in 1986. - It was an extension of his work where people could buy shirts and buttons with Haring’s designs. - The entire shop had floor-to-ceiling murals by Haring. - Some people criticized Haring for making art too commercial. - “The Pop Shop makes my art accessible. It’s about participation on a big level.”
